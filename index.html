What is masked?
Masked is a social network allowing users to connect with friends and speak their minds freely.
Unlike Twitter, or other social networks, there is no bias involved.
As long as you're not openly breaking the law, you're allowed here.

Some of our features:
Encrypted DMs. No one can access your private conversations, ever.
Pages. Do you run a company or website? Make a dedicated page through your account to keep users up to date on your projects!
Groups. Want a unique section of the site dedicated to you and your friends? Create a group!
